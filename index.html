Welcome to CSF!

The California Scholarship Federation, an esteemed honor organization that was established in 1921, offers several opportunities for community service while seeking academic success. CSF aims to honor high school students for leadership, character, scholarship, and community service. We participate in a variety of volunteer activities, such as helping out at homeless shelters, marathons, tutoring young children, and more!

This website will serve as a primary source of information for club members to find forms, track credits, and sign up for events. We appreciate the continued support of CSF from our members and the community.
